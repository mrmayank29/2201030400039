Colorful Foam Experiment

Ingredients Required:

A soda bottle, Â½ cup hydrogen peroxide liquid, dry yeast, warm water, a small cup, safety goggles, and liquid dish soap.

How to Set Up:

Put on your safety goggles and pour hydrogen peroxide liquid into a bottle. Add food color and liquid dish soap into a bottle. Shake it. In a small cup, mix warm water and yeast for 30 seconds. Finally, let your students pour the yeast mixture into a bottle to form colorful foam.
